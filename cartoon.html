Cartoon

At present I am employed as a cartoonist and writter at the assamese daily news paper Asomiya Khabor (Guwahati). I have been working for last ten years as a cartoonist alternativly Trishnatur (Assamese monthly magazine), Abikol (Comics magazine), Rahasya (Assamese monthly magazine), Rangman (Comics), Xuprovat Guwahati (Daily news paper), Adinor Sambad (Daily Assamese news paper), Asomiya Khabor (The daily Assamese newspaper).

I published already two nos cartoon books — Mubinor Nirbachito Cartoon 2012

and Mubinor Nirbasito Cartoon 2013 published by Rashang Prakshan Guwahati.

Continously I have been displaying India's first open and mobile cartoon exibition at Guwahati and various other places in north-east.
