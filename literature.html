Literature

Mor Sobi Mor Kobita is my first poetry book published by Rashang Prakashan (Guwahati) on 2012. I have written about 200 nos poetrys, 12 nos short story, 100 nos articles on social, political, cultural subjects.

I was a editor of the The Goalpara College magazine (2002). I won the best literature award, the best exhibitioner award of the college. I completed as a chief editor of the Rahba news paper Chingi Khurang for four years also.
